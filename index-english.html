Fences

Product description

You want to increase the height of your fence on an existing low wall? Opt for a rigid fence with notched posts on welded plates. Easy and quick to install, all you need is 3 lag bolts per post and you're done.

A charcoal gray or green (see Colors section) will be ideal to add modernity to your home. Conscious of the important number of construction WTB proposes you qualitative products to ennoble your habitat.
